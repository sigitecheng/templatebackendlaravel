About Us

Polibatam TalentHub is a platform that bridge students and company to find the best talent for their company.

  • Easy to use
  • Easy to find talent
  • Easy to find job & intern

Tracing students Politeknik Negeri Batam performance competency and simplify company to promote their jobs or internship vacancy to students.

Why Polibatam TalentHub

Polibatam TalentHub is a platform that bridge students and company
to find the best talent for their company.

  • 01 Accomodate Industry

    Making it easier for the Industry to promote internships and job vacancies, as well as making it easier for the Industry to find the right candidate among Polibatam Students

  • 02 Tracking Student Performance

    Tracking student performance both in lectures (course completion) and outside lectures (Extracurricular and Independent Learning Activities)

  • 03 Showing Student Portfolio

    Displays the portfolio of each student. Make it easy for the industry to find and select the best students.

  • 04 Showing Alumni Portfolio

    Displays the portfolio of each alumni. Make it easy for the industry to find and select the best alumni from polibatam.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to register intership

    Please login first with the polybatam learning account, select the internship vacancy you want to apply, fill in the required data, wait for confirmation

  • How to login to Polibatam TalentHub

    Students can login using a learning account. For Industry, you can register first in the Register section, then wait for confirmation.

  • How to Upload data to Polibatam TalentHub ?

    Go to the Profile page, from the Competence section, upload the required data.
